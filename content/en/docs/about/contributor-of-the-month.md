The Kubeflow Contributor of the Month program celebrates the people who make this project possible. From PRs merged and code to blogs, outreach and advocacy activities, this work drives the project forward. 

This program highlights the individuals who go above and beyond to support the community.

## Who is eligible?

Every contributor to the Kubeflow project is eligible for nomination. This includes all forms of contributions, which are outlined on [Contributing](/docs/about/contributing/)!

The program welcomes nominations for both long-term community members and new contributors, across multiple categories:

- New contributor
- Community Outreach & Advocacy
- Kubeflow Releases
- New Feature implementations
- Bug Fixes & Security
- Technical Documentation

## Submission process

To nominate yourself or a peer who meets the eligbility criteria, fill out this google form with the following details:

- Contributor name and country.
- Description of contribution type.
- The specific Kubeflow project or event involved.
- A link to the contribution(s). 
- A Referral/Sponsor.

The selection process prioritizes impact over volume, to highlight the significance of the contribution(s).

## Recognition

Each month, a contributor(s) from the pool of nominees will be selected for official recognition. Selected nominees will get recognition and acknowledgement through several channels:

- **Community meetings**: You will receive a mention during community meetings.
- **Social Media**: Your contributions will be highlighted on social media.
- **Kubeflow Website**: Your name gets added to a new recognition page on the Kubeflow website.
- **Speaking Engagements**: Your contributions will be mentioned during speaking engagements.
- A Digital badge for recognition.
