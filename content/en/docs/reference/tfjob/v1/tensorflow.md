<h3 id="github.com/kubeflow/tf-operator/pkg/apis/tensorflow/v1.TFReplicaType">TFReplicaType

&ldquo;Worker&rdquo;, &ldquo;PS&rdquo;, or &ldquo;Evaluator&rdquo;.</p>
